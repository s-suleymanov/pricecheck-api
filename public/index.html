Tired of overpaying? With PriceCheck you can:

1. Compare stores

Same product, different price. PriceCheck lines up Amazon, Target, Walmart, Apple, Nike, and more so you can buy the exact item for less.

  • Matches by UPC and specs
  • See shipping and taxes before you decide
  • Open the best listing in one click

2. Check codes

Public codes and store promos are tried for you. See the real total before checkout.

  • Applies best code first
  • Shows stackable promos when available
  • Clear savings, zero guesswork

3. Decide faster

See recent highs and lows. If waiting makes sense, you will know. If not, buy with confidence.

  • Recent trend line at a glance
  • Signals when a drop is likely
  • Alerts if the price spikes

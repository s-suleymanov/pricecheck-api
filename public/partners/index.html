Partners

PriceCheck uses these products and services as a customer. They do not sponsor or formally partner with PriceCheck, and their logos are used here for identification only.

Namecheap

Domains and DNS for the PriceCheck web properties. Namecheap keeps routing simple and reliable.

Render

Application hosting for the PriceCheck backend and APIs. Render makes deploys and scaling straightforward.

Neon

Serverless Postgres for product, price, and history data. Neon provides fast storage with modern branching features.

Atlassian

Project tracking and documentation across PriceCheck work. Atlassian tools help keep roadmap and tasks organized.

DBeaver

Database client used to explore, debug, and tune the PriceCheck Postgres schemas day to day.

Cloudflare

CDN and edge services that help PriceCheck load quickly and stay available for shoppers around the world.

Privacy Policy for PriceCheck Extension & Dashboard

Last updated: October 25, 2025

Thank you for choosing PriceCheck. This privacy policy explains what information our extension handles and why. Our goal is to provide a useful service while respecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

When you activate the extension on a supported product page (such as Amazon, Target, Walmart, or Best Buy) the extension script reads the following non-personal information from the page:

  • Product identifier (ASIN, TCIN, SKU, or UPC)
  • Product title and current price
  • Product page URL

This information relates only to the product being viewed and is not linked to you or your account.

How We Use Information

The product information is securely sent to the PriceCheck backend (hosted on Neon infrastructure) to match the same item at other retailers and return comparison prices. A limited product-level record (store, product ID, price, and timestamp) may be saved to build public price history data. This information is never tied to an individual user.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties. Data is used solely to provide accurate price comparison results and maintain price history analytics.

Security

All communication between the extension and our server uses HTTPS encryption. Access to backend data is limited to authorized system administrators for debugging and maintenance purposes only.
